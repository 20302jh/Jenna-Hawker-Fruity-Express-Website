Careers At Fruity Express

Are you passionate about bubble tea and providing exceptional customer service? Join the Fruity Express team and be a part of the bubble tea revolution! We are always looking for enthusiastic and friendly individuals who are dedicated to creating a delightful experience for our customers.

Available Positions

  1. - Bubble Tea Barista
  2. - Cashier
  3. - Store Manager

Benefits of Working at Fruity Express

  1. - Competitive wages
  2. - Flexible working hours
  3. - Fun and friendly work environment
  4. - Opportunities for growth and advancement
  5. - Free bubble tea during shifts

How to Apply

To apply for a position at Fruity Express, please send your resume and cover letter to careers@fruityexpress.com. Alternatively, you can call our HR department at +1-123- 456-7890 for more information.
